CS335 Projects

    What are our motivations for machine learning? Profit-motivated? Self-motivated? "Self-transcendent motivation": what can we do for others? Three application areas:
    1. Health
    2. Education
    3. Climate Change

    2-4 people in a group. No more, no less. Work should be scaled according to the number in the group.

    (1) Idea Proposal

    The Idea Proposal consists of two stages: (1) write-up your proposal idea with THIS format, and (2) read 5 other people's proposal. What are the strengths? What are the weaknesses? (3) Form groups.

    Proposal should have (1) Research question, (2) Dataset (must have a dataset), (3) Methodology ideas, (4) Ethical considerations, (5) Evaluation (performance metrics). Must contain at least one visualization!

    (2) Annotated bibliography

    This second stage, consists of finding relevant research papers to read. Think about the quality of the paper. You must include at minimum 3 * n papers where n is the number of people in your group. At least two papers can be chapters in a textbook. Provide names, years, conferences, link (provide template). For each paper, write 5-10 sentences about that paper. What are the main conclusions? See this link: https://people.cs.umass.edu/~brenocon/inlp2016/hw6/hw6.pdf

    How is your project similar or different to these studies?

    (3) Presentation

    (4) Final Report

    8-12 page research write-up.
