Katherine A. Keith

I am a PhD student in Computer Science working with Brendan O'Connor and the SLANG lab at the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

My research focuses on improving natural language processing methods for computational social science applications with unstructured text data. I'm interested in improving social measurements of text (e.g. event extraction and extracting semantic and pragmatic signals from text) and improving methods that use these measurements in descriptive and causal inferences.
